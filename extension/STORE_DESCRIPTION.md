# PW Focus: Developer Context & Safety

**Manage your environment context: Stay safe in production and focused on your tasks.**

PW Focus harmonizes your developer workflow by making your browser "context-aware." Whether you are deploying code or debugging a complex issue, PW Focus ensures you have the right guardrails and the right resources at the right time.

Stop drowning in tabs. Define your "Focus" (e.g., "Deployment", "Q3 Planning") and let the extension organize your browser.
